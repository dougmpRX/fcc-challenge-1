Dr. Albert W. Wily

The man who tried to destroy the world a dozen times, and still counting...

    Here is a time line of Dr. Albert W. Wily

  • 20XX - Worked with Dr. Thomas Light in robot science lab
  • 20XX - Modified Proto Man, turning him into Mega Man's rival
  • 20XX - Turned Dr. Light's creations into evil, now called Robot Masters
  • 20XX - Finishes the work of his life, a robot carrying a powerful virus capable of turning any other into evil
  • 21XX - No one knows his whereabouts, but his creation, now called Sigma Virus is the biggest threat in the world.
